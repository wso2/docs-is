SDKs

{{ config.extra.product_name }} simplifies application integration by offering Software Development Kits (SDKs) that handle authentication in your apps. These SDKs abstract away protocol-level complexities, making it easier and faster for developers to connect their applications to {{ config.extra.product_name }}.

{{ config.extra.product_name }} officially supports a set of popular programming languages and frameworks by providing and maintaining its own SDKs. These SDKs are kept up-to-date, tested for reliability, and supported as part of the platform.

For other languages and platforms that do not yet have an official SDK, developers can use community-maintained OAuth/OIDC SDKs to integrate with {{ config.extra.product_name }}. While these SDKs are not maintained by {{ config.extra.product_name }}, they can still offer a reliable way to connect applications.

We are continuously working on expanding official SDK support to cover as many languages and frameworks as possible.
