Before you start, ensure you have the following:

- About 60 minutes of time.
- A WSO2 Identity Platform account.
- Create [a test user in WSO2 Identity Platform](https://wso2.com/asgardeo/docs/guides/users/manage-users/#onboard-users)
- [.NET 8 SDK](https://dotnet.microsoft.com/en-us/download){:target="_blank"} or later.
- [Visual Studio Code](https://code.visualstudio.com/).
