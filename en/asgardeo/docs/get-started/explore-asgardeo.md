## What is WSO2 Identity Platform?

WSO2 Identity Platform is an identity as a service (IDaaS) solution that provides a fully integrated developer experience to create seamless login experiences in minutes. You can get started with common IAM use cases with no installation or maintenance hassles.

## Self-service portals (My Account)

The **My Account** portal is the self-service portal for WSO2 Identity Platform users. If you are an owner of an WSO2 Identity Platform of one or multiple WSO2 Identity Platform organization, you can manage your personal profile from this portal.

Customers of your organizations will have their own **My Account** portal to manage their personal accounts.
